Budget Calculator

Purpose and Recommended Use:

This is a tool which shows the distribution of expenses on different categories. It was built with monthly expenses in mind.

Instructions:

You will see a table with three colums.
One has a category name, the second has an input box to put the allocation in, and the last tracks the percentage of total allocations taken by that allocation.
Like the Budget Helper, you can add and delete categories at will.
