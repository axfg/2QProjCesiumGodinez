What is Basic Finance Tools?

Basic Finance Tools is a website that contains several tools that can help you with your personal finance. These tools can help one to visualize and manage one's personal finance habits. This website is for people who are completely new to personal finance, since the tools shown here help only with the fundamentals. Also, there are much better alternatives to this website.

You may visit the "About" page via the navigation bar to find out more about the website.

Tools

These are the tools found in the website. You may access them through either the navigation bar or by clicking their respective boxes.

Savings Calculator

This helps you calculate your potential earnings from interest.

Budget Helper

This tool can help you plan a budget which can account for spending limits on different categories.

Expenses Visualizer

This tool presents your expenses into a more readable form, giving you a better grip of your expense distribution.
